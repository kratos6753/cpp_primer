difference between int, long, long long, short is the range of values they support
let's say, 
int is 2 or 4 bytes
long is 4 bytes
long long is 8 bytes
short is 2 bytes

difference between signed and unsigned type
in unsigned type, the range covers only positive numbers
in signed type, the range covers both positive and negative numbers

difference between float and double
float is 4 bytes
double is 8 bytes
